What we Offer

Sportletics offers a diverse range of programs to engage students in developping self confidence, physical literacy and lifelong healthy-living habits. We focus on helping students develop awareness and patience, while constantly challenging them to perform at their personal best. We provide a fun, supportive, and respectful environment for all students.

Martial Arts

Martial arts classes aren't just about punching and kicking - they are about develoing happy, strong and healthy citizens. The Sportletics martial arts program imporoves self-confidence, self-esteem, focus, balance, coordination and more. Our instructors will deliver crucial self-defence skills to students in a fun, supportuve and respectful environment.
